02 — About

About
the Work

“A slower, sharper way of looking at the world.”

I’m Ruben Van Ruysseveldt, a photographer based in Belgium. This is a personal space — built to share my work with myself and with the people I meet along the way. Nothing commercial; just a love of photography in all its forms.

Lately I’m drawn most to film. There’s something about it that holds an emotion you can’t fake — entirely in the moment. It asks you to step into the scene yourself, and in doing so it changes how you experience life: a slower, sharper, different way of looking at the world.
